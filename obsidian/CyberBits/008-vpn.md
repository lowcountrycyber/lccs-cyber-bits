# VPN (Virtual Private Network)

**One-liner:** A VPN creates an encrypted tunnel so remote staff can securely connect into your office network or cloud systems.

**Why it matters for small businesses / home offices:** Without a proper VPN, staff often use risky workarounds from home or public Wi-Fi that expose your data.
