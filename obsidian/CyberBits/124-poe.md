# Power over Ethernet (PoE)

**One-liner:** PoE sends power and data over one Ethernet cable to devices like phones or cameras.

**Why it matters for small businesses / home offices:** PoE simplifies installs and keeps phones or cameras up during power issues when paired with a UPS.
