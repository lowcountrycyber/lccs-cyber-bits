# Wide Area Network (WAN)

**One-liner:** A WAN links multiple sites or remote users over the internet or private circuits.

**Why it matters for small businesses / home offices:** Your WAN quality drives how usable remote offices and cloud apps feel.

**Related:** [[VPN (Virtual Private Network)]], [[Internet Service Provider (ISP)]], [[Router]]
