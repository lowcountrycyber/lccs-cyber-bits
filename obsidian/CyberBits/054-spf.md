# SPF

**One-liner:** SPF is a DNS record that lists which servers can send email for your domain.

**Why it matters for small businesses / home offices:** SPF reduces spoofed mail that can trick staff or customers.

**Related:** [[DKIM]], [[DMARC]], [[Email Filtering]]
